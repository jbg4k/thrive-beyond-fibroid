Silo Mentalities in Rural Areas

Breaking down silo mentalities in rural healthcare fosters collaboration and improves fibroid care. Through partnerships and education, we aim to ensure comprehensive care for rural fibroid patients.

Patient Advocacy

We advocate for fibroid patients, amplifying their voices and addressing their needs. Our campaigns raise awareness, promote informed decision-making, and push for policies prioritizing patient well-being.

Sensitization to Younger Percentage of Women

Raising awareness among younger women about fibroids is crucial. We educate through targeted campaigns in schools and online platforms, empowering early recognition and proactive management.

Male Inclusivity in Maternal Health Discussions

Including men in fibroid discussions fosters understanding and support. We engage partners and healthcare providers to ensure holistic care and advocacy for affected individuals.

Affordable Surgical Procedures

TEnsuring access to affordable surgical options is vital for fibroid patients. We advocate for innovative, cost-effective approaches to reduce financial barriers and improve treatment accessibility.

Research Advancement

Driving fibroid research is essential for better understanding and treatment. We support collaborative efforts to accelerate research progress and translate findings into clinical practice.
